      Analysis and commentary

      CIO Monthly Letter 

      16 April 2014: The dog that barks?

      • Inflation has remained remarkably stable in the wake of the 2008-09 financial crisis.
      • Inflationary dynamics are now becoming more challenging, albeit in different ways across regions.
      • US employment data suggests we should see higher inflation this year. Themarket’s focus on rising Fed rates should result in higher Treasury yields and a stronger US dollar.
      • The Eurozone’s flirtation with deflation has transformed the perceived limits
        of ECB policy, and we add to our overweight position in equities.
