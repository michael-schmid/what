goals

These are some of the goals we want to achieve with code52:

  • introduce developers to open-source projects and help them learn the ropes
  • contribute to projects across a range of technologies
  • let contributors have a say in what we do each week

The current organisers of code52 are Andrew Tobin, Brendan Forster and Paul Jenkins.

what can I do?

The easiest way to get started is to dive straight in:
  • suggest a project
  • chatroom
  • twitter
  • contribute to a previous project
